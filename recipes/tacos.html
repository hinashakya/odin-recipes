Tacos recipe

Description:

Tacos are a versatile and flavorful Mexican dish made by filling soft or crispy tortillas with various ingredients. You can customize them with meats, beans, vegetables, and toppings like salsa, cheese, and avocado. Theyâ€™re perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef, chicken, or turkey
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning (or to taste)
  • 8 small tortillas (corn or flour)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Salsa or pico de gallo
  • Sour cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Meat: In a skillet over medium heat, brown the ground meat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water, stirring to combine. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  2. Warm the Tortillas: Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until warm and pliable.
  3. Assemble the Tacos: Place a spoonful of the seasoned meat in each tortilla. Top with lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, avocado, and any other desired toppings.
  4. Serve: Serve immediately with salsa and sour cream on the side.
