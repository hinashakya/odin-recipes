Lasagna recipe


Classic lasagna features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella cheese. It's baked until bubbly and golden, making for a hearty, comforting dish perfect for family gatherings or cozy dinners. Enjoy it with a side salad and garlic bread!

For the meet sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the Cheese Mixture:
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For Assembly
  • 12 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or boiled)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan for topping

Steps :

  1. Make the Meat Sauce: Sauté onion and garlic, add ground beef until browned, then stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix the Cheese: Combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Preheat Oven: Set to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Assemble: In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, noodles, half the ricotta, and a third of the mozzarella. Repeat, then top with remaining noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and extra Parmesan.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until bubbly.
  6. Cool and Serve: Let sit for 15 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
